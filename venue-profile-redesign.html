About The Venue

The Basement has been Brooklyn's premier underground house music venue since 2018. Located in the heart of Bushwick, we've built our reputation on showcasing both emerging local talent and internationally recognized DJs in an intimate, high-energy setting.

Our state-of-the-art Funktion-One sound system and custom-designed acoustics create an immersive experience that keeps dancers coming back week after week. We pride ourselves on fostering a diverse, inclusive community where the music and the vibe are always the priority.

We're looking for promoters who understand underground dance music culture and can bring authentic energy to our space. Ideal events run from 10 PM to 4 AM on Fridays and Saturdays, with capacity for 300 people.
