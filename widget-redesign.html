For Venue Owners: Embed VenueLink on Your Site

Copy and paste this simple code snippet anywhere on your website to add the VenueLink booking widget. It's as easy as adding a Resy or OpenTable integration.

<!-- VenueLink Booking Widget -->
<div id="venuelink-booking"></div>
<script src="https://venuelink.com/widget.js"></script>
<script>
  VenueLink.init({
    venueId: 'your-venue-id',
    theme: 'dark', // 'light', 'dark', or 'auto'
    accentColor: '#fbbf24' // Optional: match your brand
  });
</script>
