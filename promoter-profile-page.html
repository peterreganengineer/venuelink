About Brooklyn Bass Collective

Brooklyn Bass Collective has been bringing underground house music to NYC since 2020. We specialize in deep house, minimal techno, and bass-heavy grooves with a focus on both established international talent and emerging local DJs.

Our events consistently draw 300-400 attendees who are there for the music - a mature, respectful crowd aged 25-40 who appreciate proper sound systems and quality production. We've built a reputation for discovering talent early and creating intimate, high-energy experiences.

We handle all aspects of event promotion in-house, including social media campaigns, email marketing, artist collaboration, and street-level buzz. Our average presale performance is 60% of tickets sold in the first week, with most events selling out 2-3 days before doors.
